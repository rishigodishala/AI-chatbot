Emotional Intelligence (EI) involves self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills.

Self-awareness: Recognizing one's own emotions and their impact.

Self-regulation: Managing emotions and impulses effectively.

Motivation: Harnessing emotions to achieve goals.

Empathy: Understanding others' emotions and perspectives.

Social skills: Building and maintaining relationships.

High EI is crucial in recruitment for roles involving leadership, teamwork, and customer interaction.
